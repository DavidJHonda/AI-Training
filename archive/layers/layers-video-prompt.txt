Read it a third time and it clicks: a horse ran past a barn — and after passing it, the horse fell. Each pass, your mind updates the meaning. AI does the same thing. It reads your message over and over, a little more meaning each pass — nuance takes dozens of passes to pin down. Each pass is called a layer, and at every layer AI runs the two moves you met in the Transformer lesson: attention — which words matter — and transformation — update the meaning.
