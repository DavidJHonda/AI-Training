> **For agentic workers:** REQUIRED SUB-SKILL: Use superpowers:subagent-driven-development (recommended) or superpowers:executing-plans to implement this plan task-by-task. Steps use checkbox (`- [ ]`) syntax for tracking.

**Goal:** Add a new "AI Primer" lesson as the first content lesson of Understand AI that installs the whole how-AI-works loop in plain language, and fold the standalone Probability lesson into it (section stays at 16 lessons).

**Architecture:** Single-file inline-React app (`index.html`, `React.createElement`, no JSX/build). Lessons are functions registered in three structures: `SECTION_GROUPS` (order + clustering, ~line 1206), `SECTION_META` (kicker/label/icon, ~line 1228), and `SECTION_COMPONENTS` (id → component, ~line 13534). `SECTIONS` is derived from these. The Primer reuses the removed Probability lesson's SEE IT bar chart and "Guess the Most Probable Word" TRY IT, re-expressed without "weights"/"token" vocabulary (those debut later in the section).

**Tech Stack:** HTML + inline React (createElement) + design tokens (CSS custom properties). Verification gates: `bash design-check.sh`, the in-app `validate()` consistency checker (runs on load, logs to console), and manual browser render. There is no unit-test framework; do NOT invent one.

**Spec:** `docs/superpowers/specs/2026-06-04-ai-primer-design.md`

**Global copy rules (apply to every line of new Primer copy):**
- Everyday language; "what not how." No mechanism.
- Do NOT use the words **"weights"** or **"token"** (they debut in Training/Tokens, which come *after* the Primer). Say "the next **word**," "what it **learned**," "the patterns it **stored**."
- No em-dashes. `design-check.sh` baselines em-dashes at 4; any new one FLAGs. Rewrite without them.
- No false closure. Do NOT end with "now you understand how AI works." End with "now you've got the shape; the rest of this section opens up each part."
- Reuse components (`BodyP`, `SectionKicker`, `ShowcaseBox`, `InteractiveBox`, `InnerCard`, `ActivityCounter`, `KeyInsight`, `NextLessonGate`). Do NOT hand-build counter pills or raw surfaces (would FLAG design-check).
